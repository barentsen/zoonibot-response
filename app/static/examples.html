ZooniBot response examples

This #dotastro hack gives the ZooniBot the bare minimum of intelligence needed to respond to common questions posted on Galaxy Zoo and Planet Hunters Talk.

Galaxy Zoo example

GalaxyZoo Talk user: "the top left has something interesting that looks interesting, but i could just be stupid".

ZooniBot's response: http://zoonibot.geert.io:4242/gz_summary?RA=102.84089&DEC=39.192889&SR=0.01

Planet Hunters example

PlanetHunters talk user: is APH10112880 a planet candidate?

ZooniBot's response: http://zoonibot.geert.io:4242/ph_candidate?RA=291.04299927&DEC=36.59379959
